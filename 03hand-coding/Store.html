terms to know

Exhibition
A curated collection of artworks displayed together, often centered around a specific theme, artist, or time period.
Medium
The material or technique used by an artist to create a work of art, such as oil paint, watercolor, sculpture, or digital media.
Gallery
A space dedicated to the display and appreciation of visual art, where visitors can view and sometimes purchase pieces.
