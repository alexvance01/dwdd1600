What is a Comission?

So… you’re looking to create a stunning portrait, capture a special moment, or simply find something unique to elevate your space. You’ve come to the right place — there’s no better way to bring your vision to life than through paint.
Commissioned artwork is a custom, one-of-a-kind piece created just for you. It’s a chance to collaborate with an artist to design something that reflects your story, your style, and your space. Whether it’s a gift, a centerpiece for your home, or a way to preserve a meaningful memory, a commissioned painting transforms your ideas into a lasting work of art.
When you commission a piece from me, you’ll receive a personalized, hand-painted oil painting tailored to your preferences and specifications. I take great pride in my craft and strive to make the entire process seamless and enjoyable — from our first conversation to the final brushstroke.
